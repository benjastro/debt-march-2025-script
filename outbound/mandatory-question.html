Debt Script - Outbound

Mandatory Quesstion for DEBT

  1. How much do you currently owe in credit card or unsecured debt?
    Is it over $10,000 or $15,000? (Must be $10,000 or more to qualify)

  2. Are you currently in bankruptcy? If yes, has it been discharged?
    (Must be NO to qualify)

  3. Are you enrolled in any type of debt consolidation or settlement program?
    (Must be NO to qualify)

  4. Do you have any source of income? MUST BE YES
